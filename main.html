Name of the Destination: Maldives


Brief Overview: The Maldives, an Indian Ocean paradise, is renowned for its stunning coral reefs, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and overwater bungalows.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Snorkeling at Banana Reef: Explore vibrant coral gardens and encounter diverse marine life.
  2. Dhigurah Island: A local island with pristine beaches and a glimpse into Maldivian culture.
  3. Maafushi Bikini Beach: Relax on the white sandy beaches and soak up the sun.
  4. Male Atoll: Visit the capital city for bustling markets and historical sites.
  5. Underwater Restaurant Ithaa: Dine with panoramic views of marine life.

Must-Try Foods

  • Mas Huni: A traditional Maldivian breakfast dish made with tuna, coconut, onion, and chili.
  • Fihunu Mas: Grilled fish seasoned with Maldivian spices for a burst of flavor.
  • Garudhiya: A fish soup often enjoyed with rice, lime, chili, and onions.
  • Rihaakuru: A thick fish paste used as a condiment in many Maldivian dishes.
  • Kulhi Boakibaa: A savory fish cake, a popular snack in the Maldives.
